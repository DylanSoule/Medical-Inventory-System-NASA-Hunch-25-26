The Problem

Understanding the challenges we aim to solve with H.E.L.I.O.S

Astronauts aboard the International Space Station (ISS) often utilize medical supplies without properly logging their usage. This oversight can lead to inaccurate inventory counts, which poses significant risks during long-duration missions where resupply opportunities are limited. NASA requires a user-friendly system that can accurately track medical supplies in real-time and transmit this data back to Earth. Such a system is essential for effective resupply planning and ensuring that astronauts have access to necessary medical resources when needed.
